Get to know what we are all about.

Our Athletic program with Basketball as the blueprint is specialized in customized individual player development including;

  • Basketball & Football Training.
  • Trauma Healing Sessions.
  • In-Class Support.
  • Basketball for Beginners.
  • Accommodation through Our Fully Funded Scholarships.

Our Blossoms of Hope is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering girls through basketball, and football and providing them with opportunities for education, trauma healing, and a safe place to call home. We work with girls from the Kibera slums, nomadic families from Turkana, and the Maasai community, who are often exposed to early marriage, female genital mutilation (FGM), and other forms of gender-based violence. Through our comprehensive program, we offer girls a chance to break free from the cycle of poverty and violence and reach their full potential. Our basketball and football program provides them with physical and mental training, while our trauma healing workshops help them address the emotional scars of their past experiences. In addition, we offer in-class support to help them succeed academically and provide them with a safe place to sleep through our fully funded scholarships.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable girls from the Kibera slums, nomadic families in Turkana, and the Maasai community through our holistic program, combining basketball and football training, trauma healing sessions, academic support, and safe housing. Our goal is to break the cycle of poverty and gender-based violence, fostering the holistic development of these girls and enabling them to become leaders in their communities.

Objectives

Physical and Mental Empowerment: Emotional Resilience and Recovery: Academic Success and Skill Development: Safe and Supportive Environment:

Our Vision

A world where every girl, empowered through education and sports, breaks free from adversity, becomes a leader, and inspires positive change in her community.

Target Population:

Demographic and Socio-Economic Characteristics

  • Kibera Slums

  • Nomadic Families in Turkana

  • Maasai Community (FGM Survivors)

Kibera Slums:

- Demographic Profile: Primarily urban, with a mix of ethnicities.
- Socio-Economic Context: Families in Kibera often face extreme poverty, limited access to basic amenities, and overcrowded living conditions. Educational opportunities are limited, perpetuating a cycle of generational poverty.

  • Limited Educational Opportunities:
    Girls often face barriers to education due to poverty and inadequate infrastructure.
  • Exposure to Gender-Based Violence:
    High levels of gender-based violence pose a threat to the physical and emotional well-being of girls.

Nomadic Families in Turkana:

- Demographic Profile: Predominantly from nomadic pastoralist communities.
- Socio-Economic Context: Nomadic lifestyles pose challenges to consistent access to education and healthcare. Economic hardships, exacerbated by climate-related issues, impact the overall well-being of families.

  • Limited Access to Education:
    Nomadic lifestyles make consistent attendance challenging, limiting educational opportunities.
  • Economic Hardships:
    Families face economic challenges, impacting the overall well-being of girls and their ability to pursue education.

Maa Community (FGM Survivors):

- Demographic Profile: Indigenous Maasai communities.
- Socio-Economic Context: Girls who have fled from FGM face unique challenges, including social ostracization and the need to rebuild their lives. Access to education and healthcare is often limited, and cultural norms may perpetuate gender-based discrimination.

  • Fleeing from FGM:
    Girls who have escaped FGM seek refuge from harmful cultural practices, requiring support to rebuild their lives.
  • Limited Access to Support Services:
    Cultural norms may limit access to essential services, including education and healthcare.

Program Overview: Empowering Holistic Development

1. Basketball and Football Training:

Physical and Mental Empowerment.

  • 4:00pm-6:00pm Weekdays.
  • 2:00pm-6:00pm Every Saturday & Sunday.
  • Through sports, we instill not only athletic skills but also vital life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and resilience. Engaging in basketball and football fosters physical well-being, boosts self-confidence, and cultivates a sense of achievement, laying the foundation for a healthy and active lifestyle.
Contact Us

2. Trauma Healing Sessions:

Emotional Resilience and Recovery.

  • 1hr Session Every Wednesday.
  • Recognizing the impact of past traumatic experiences, our trauma healing sessions provide a safe space for girls to express themselves, heal, and build emotional resilience. By addressing the emotional scars, we empower them to navigate challenges with strength and move forward positively.
More Info

3. In-Class Support:

Academic Success and Skill Development.

  • Education is a key pillar in breaking the cycle of poverty. Our in-class support ensures that girls receive the academic assistance they need to excel. Beyond academic achievement, we focus on skill development, preparing them for a future where they can contribute meaningfully to their communities.
More Info

4. Accommodation through Fully Funded Scholarships:

Safe and Supportive Environment.

  • Recognizing the importance of a stable home environment, Blossoms of Hope provides fully funded scholarships that include safe accommodation. This removes barriers to education and ensures that the girls have a secure and supportive place to call home, fostering a sense of belonging and stability.
